FAQ

Straight answers.

Is the terminal actually live, or fake?

Actually live. It runs in your browser and queries Robinhood Chain directly — new pools from GeckoTerminal, contract bytecode and balances via the chain RPC, holders and transfers via Blockscout. Every token and verdict you see is computed from real on-chain data at that moment. Refresh and it re-scans the latest launches.

Is this affiliated with Robinhood?

No. $AGENT is an independent project and a work of commentary, not affiliated with Robinhood Markets, Inc. It's built on Robinhood Chain and inspired by Vlad Tenev's public "agentic trading" vision.

Is it financial advice?

No. The agent surfaces on-chain data and signals — it does not tell you what to buy, and nothing here is financial advice. Signals are information, not instructions.

Why hold to use it?

Access is gated by your $AGENT balance, read on-chain — no subscription, no custody. It aligns the tool with the token: the more useful the agent, the more people hold to use it.
